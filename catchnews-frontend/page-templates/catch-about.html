BRAND STORY

Catch is a contemporary new digital platform about the ideas and events shaping the world. It aims to filter and provide news-on-the-run for an impatient new generation. But it will also offer context and insight for influencers and the deeper consumer of news. When opinions are shrill and polarised, we hope to create a middle ground and build bridges. When there is set thinking, we hope to stand apart and go against the wind.We are committed to the founding values of the Constitution and ideas of social equity and justice. But our content will range over everything, from hard news to hard rock. Part of the highly-regarded Rajasthan Patrika group, Catch quite simply hopes to curate a space a voice you will grow to like and trust. We will reflect a dual personality: challenging but constructive, eclectic but mainstream, provocative but playful.The world is complex, exciting, layered, evolving, always interesting. We hope to be the same.
